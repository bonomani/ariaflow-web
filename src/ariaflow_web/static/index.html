Bandwidth Policy

Reserve bandwidth and control download concurrency
Downlink free (%)
Reserve this percentage of measured downlink. Downloads capped to the remainder. Default: 20%.
Downlink free (absolute)
Always reserve at least this many Mbps downlink. Stricter of % and absolute wins. Default: 0.
Uplink free (%)
Reserve this percentage of measured uplink. Default: 50%.
Uplink free (absolute)
Always reserve at least this many Mbps uplink. Default: 0.
Probe interval
Seconds between automatic bandwidth probes. Default: 180s.
Simultaneous downloads
Limit how many downloads ariaflow may keep active at once. Default is 1 for sequential downloads.
Duplicate active transfer
When aria2 exposes duplicate active jobs for the same URL, choose the action.
